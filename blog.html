Why I Created EchoStudy

April 2025 By Ruhika Goel

The Struggle is Real

I struggle with studying. I get distracted easily and sometimes struggle to keep track of what I need to do, how much time I need, and how much time I've already spent. Like many students, I've tried various tools and methods to organize my academic life.

The Search for Solutions

My journey led me through numerous productivity apps and tools:

  • Notion - Too complex and overwhelming
  • Phone Notes - Too basic and unstructured
  • Sticky Notes - Easy to lose track of
  • Notepad - Lacks organization features
  • Google Sheets - Too rigid and time-consuming

Nothing has worked for me. Nothing feels like it was made with students in mind.

The Birth of EchoStudy

That's why I created EchoStudy - a tool specifically designed for students who want to improve their study habits and academic performance. I wanted to create something that understands the unique challenges students face and provides practical solutions to overcome them.
